About Grand Hôtel Bois de Boulogne

Welcome

Nestled in pine‑forested grounds of around 24,000 m² in Dhour El Choueir at approximately 1,200 m elevation, Grand Hôtel Bois de Boulogne provides a peaceful mountain escape just 30–40 km from Beirut and the international airport. It is situated in a serene location, halfway between Beirut and the Bekaa valley, and about 8 km from Zaarour ski slopes.

History & Character

Established in the 1930s and later renovated, the hotel blends classic elegance with contemporary comfort. With rustic charm, spacious rooms, tasteful decoration, and tranquil ambiance, it is ideal for both leisure and business stays.

Guest Rooms & Accommodation

The hotel features around 78 – 100 guest rooms and 12 suites/apartments, some with separate living space or kitchen—suitable for long stays or families. Guests often praise the spaciousness, quiet atmosphere, and clean, comfortable beds.

Amenities & Facilities

  • Free Wi‑Fi throughout the property
  • 24‑hour reception, concierge, luggage storage, news service
  • On‑site restaurant + buffet brunch on Sundays
  • Simple bar / lounge with fireplace
  • Children’s playground / play zone (ages ~3–10)
  • Business centre, meeting room, fax/photocopy services
  • Parking (private & free), airport shuttle (surcharge)
  • Laundry, dry‑cleaning, ironing, daily housekeeping
  • Heating and air conditioning in rooms
  • Terrace, garden, communal lounge/TV

The hotel is well suited to families, business travelers, and those seeking events or private functions in a natural setting.
